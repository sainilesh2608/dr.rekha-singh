Our Treatments

Comprehensive Skin & Hair Care

From advanced laser therapies to bespoke skincare regimens — discover our full spectrum of dermatological solutions.

Acne & Scar Therapy

Clinically proven protocols to clear active acne and dramatically reduce scarring — restoring smooth, even-toned skin.

  • Chemical Peels (AHA/BHA/TCA)
  • Microneedling & Dermabrasion
  • Subcision & Punch Excision
  • Comedone Extraction
⚡

Laser Treatments

State-of-the-art laser systems for hair removal, skin resurfacing, pigmentation correction, and tattoo removal.

  • Diode Laser Hair Removal
  • Fractional CO₂ Resurfacing
  • Q-Switch Nd:YAG Laser
  • Laser Toning & Skin Brightening
💆

Hair Loss & PRP

Advanced trichology-backed treatments targeting the root cause of hair thinning, dandruff, and alopecia.

  • PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) Therapy
  • GFC (Growth Factor Concentrate)
  • Mesotherapy for Hair
  • Scalp Analysis & Treatment
🌿

Pigmentation & Brightening

Targeted therapies for melasma, dark spots, uneven skin tone, and post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation.

  • Melasma & Dark Spot Treatment
  • IV Glutathione Therapy
  • Skin Lightening Peels
  • Dark Circles Correction

Anti-Aging & Aesthetics

Luxurious non-surgical aesthetic treatments to reverse visible ageing and rejuvenate your appearance.

  • Botox & Fillers
  • HydraFacial & Insta Glow

  • Skin Boosters (Profhilo)

Meet Your Specialist

A Name Synonymous with Dermatological Excellence

Dr. T N Rekha Singh is a highly decorated Consultant Dermatologist & Cosmetologist with over two decades of expertise in aesthetic dermatology, advanced lasers, and trichology. She completed her MBBS from Kakatiya Medical College, Warangal and her MD DVL from Gandhi Medical College, Hyderabad.

A prolific academician, she has published in leading journals including the Indian Journal of Dermatology and has trained over 90 dermato-trichologists nationwide. She has chaired and presented at numerous national and international dermatology conferences.
